The Best Vegan Broccoli tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 Head Broccoli
  • 1 C. Non-Dairy Milk
  • 2 tsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 C. Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 1/2 C. BBQ Sauce
  • 1/4 C. Brown Sugar or Coconut Sugar
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • Juice of 1 Lime
  • Tortillas
  • Cilantro
  • Cabbage
  • Vegan Green Goddess Dressing

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425oF.
  2. Then cut the head of broccoli into small florets. Set aside.
  3. Set out 3 different small mixing bowls and add the following to each:
    • Bowl 1: Add the almond milk and apple cider vinegar, whisk.
    • Bowl 2: Add flour, salt, and pepper, whisk.
    • Bowl 3: Add panko.
  4. Next, take a broccoli floret and dip into the bowls in thw following order:
    1. Almond milk mixture
    2. Flour mixture - coating completely, shake off the excess
    3. Almond milk again
    4. Panko - coating completely
  5. Spray a baking sheet with non-stick spray. Place the broccoli on the baking sheet. Repeat with all the broccoli. Spray the top of the broccoli with more non-stick spray.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the broccoli is brown and crispy. Flip halfway through.
  7. While the broccoli is baking, make the sauce.
  8. In a small sauce pan, heat the BBQ sauce, sugar, garlic powder, and lime juice on medium low. Whisk together and bring to a simmer.
  9. Reduce heat to low and simmer until the sugar has dissolved.
  10. Remove from heat.
  11. Once the broccoli is done, toss the broccoli in the sauce - coating completely.
  12. Assemble the tacos: broccoli, cilantro, cabbage, and dressing in the tortilla.
  13. Enjoy!
