Basics of Dog Walking Businesses

Certifications

Step 1 - Get Certified!

  • CPR for Dogs
  • Business License
  • Waivers created
  • Basic Dog Training License



Step 2 - Acquire Clients

Use your existing connections to start a client base. From there, request your friends and current clients refer you to others you you can continue to grow your business.

Try offering bonuses or discounts for referrals!




Step 3 - Start walkin'!

Enjoy your new job walking the neighborhood dogs! Be sure to ask and be aware of any behavioral issues or preferences of your clients dogs. Keep lots of water and treats (if allowed) on hand and have fun!
